## Definition
Content Velocity is an AI-powered publication analysis engine that tracks publishing patterns, sitemap output, and competitive velocity.

## How it works
Analyzes all URLs in the sitemap and plots publication timing, frequency, and category output. Reveals trends, gaps, and seasonal patterns.

## Data views
- Day-level publishing trends
- Monthly distribution charts
- Yearly summaries
- Growth comparisons against competitors

## Core capabilities
- Filtering by sitemap, content category, date range, or competitor domain
- Long-term publishing cycle visualization
- Identification of momentum periods
- Competitor benchmarking

## Integrations
- Content Genius
- Keyword Research
- Indexation tracking
- Site Audit
