## Definition
Keyword Gap Tool is a competitive analysis feature that uncovers keywords competitors rank for but the target domain does not capture. Compares up to 6 domains side by side.

## How it works
Analyzes Monthly Search Volume (MSV), SERP positions, and search intent. Displays ranking comparisons and position-based filters (All Positions, Top 10) alongside competitor distribution charts. Keyword lists sort by search volume, ranking position, and traffic potential.

## View segments
- Keyword Gap: terms competitors rank for that the target domain misses
- Keywords in Common: shared ranking terms across analyzed domains
- Keyword Opportunities: high-potential terms with favorable difficulty scores
- Unique Keywords: terms where only one domain ranks
- All Keywords: comprehensive list across all domains

## Core capabilities
- Side-by-side comparison across up to 6 domains
- Position-based filters (All Positions, Top 10)
- Sortable keyword lists (search volume, ranking position, traffic potential)

## Integrations
- Content Genius
- Portfolio Summary
