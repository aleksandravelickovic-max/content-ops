## Definition
Content Pruning Tool is an AI-powered content evaluation engine that identifies low-quality, thin, outdated, or underperforming pages across a domain.

## How it works
Analyzes every page in the domain against organic impressions, clicks, indexability status, ranking keywords, content score, and average position. Groups URLs into pruning categories.

## Signals used
- Organic impressions
- Clicks
- Indexability status
- Ranking keywords
- Content score
- Average position

## Core capabilities
- Filtering by performance, indexability, keyword count, or content quality
- Automatic grouping into actionable pruning categories (low-impression URLs, thin content, outdated resources, duplicate content, weak ranking signals)
- Pruning paths: rewrites, redirects, consolidations, removals

## Integrations
- Content Genius
- Onpage Audit Tool
- Backlink Research
- Internal linking recommendations
