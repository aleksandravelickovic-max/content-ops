## Definition
AI Content Templates is a modular AI generation feature that produces optimized marketing copy across multiple channels (SEO, email, social, ads) using structured prompt engineering and semantic intent modeling.

## How it works
Applies natural language generation (NLG), keyword mapping, and entity correlation. Each template is pre-trained on linguistic patterns specific to its use case.

## Categories (8)
- SEO
- Blog
- Ads
- Email
- Ecommerce
- Google My Business
- Video
- Social Media

Each category maps to over 60 pre-engineered prompt templates.

## Core capabilities
- Tone and perspective customization (informative, persuasive, casual)
- Keyword weighting for ranking and engagement
- Outputs aligned with Google NLP and OpenAI linguistic constraints
