## Definition
LLM Visibility Tool is an AI-powered brand visibility engine in Search Atlas that identifies brand mentions, tracks sentiment shifts, and uncovers visibility gaps across ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and Perplexity.

## How it works
Analyzes any domain, brand, or topic. Generates AI-specific insights: visibility percentages, sentiment evaluations, citation sources, and competitor benchmarks.

## Data views
- Visibility trends
- Share-of-voice metrics
- Cross-platform ranking comparisons
- Sentiment patterns
- Topic-level performance

## Core capabilities
- Instant monitoring across multiple domains, platforms, and competitors
- Automatic grouping of citations, topics, and sentiment drivers into dashboards
- Cross-model visibility scoring
- Multi-model sentiment tracking

## Integrations
- Digital PR ecosystem
- Publisher Exchange
- QUEST
