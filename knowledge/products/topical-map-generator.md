## Definition
Topical Map Generator is a semantic clustering tool in Search Atlas that builds topic-based SEO roadmaps using entity relationships, keyword hierarchies, and long-tail search intent analysis.

## How it works
Transforms a single seed keyword into a complete content structure. Applies entity mapping, semantic co-occurrence modeling, and long-tail keyword expansion to identify subtopics. Outputs pillar-cluster-supporting page relationships.

## Output formats
- Interactive diagram (visual representation)
- Spreadsheet format (workflow handoff to writers or agencies)

## Core capabilities
- Seed-to-map semantic expansion
- Pillar, cluster, and supporting page hierarchy
- Category examples: Landing Page Best Practices, Conversion Rate Optimization, A/B Testing Strategies

## Integrations
- Bulk AI Article Writer (one-click article production per map item)
- Content Genius
