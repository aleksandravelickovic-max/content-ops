## Definition
URL Indexer is an AI-assisted indexing accelerator that speeds up search engine discovery through rapid submission workflows.

## How it works
Processes submitted URLs through enhanced indexing signals: structured metadata preparation, crawl-request optimization, and prioritization patterns.

## Core capabilities
- Instant batch submissions for blogs, landing pages, product pages, or site updates
- Priority signaling and crawl-readiness analysis
- Scheduled URL processing

## Integrations
- Site Audit
- Backlink Research
- Content Genius
