## Definition
Link Building Outreach and Digital PR Tool is an outreach automation system inside Search Atlas that centralizes email sequencing, follow-up scheduling, response tracking, link prospecting, and media pitching. Replaces disjointed workflows across CRMs, spreadsheets, and inboxes.

## How it works
The prospecting engine identifies domains based on traffic metrics (monthly visitor volume, engagement rate), authority scores (Domain Rating, trust flow, link equity), category (industry relevance, topical alignment), and region (geographic market, local SEO value). Automated filters enforce quality thresholds for spam score, authority level, and content alignment.

## Core capabilities
- Prospecting engine with filtering by traffic, authority, category, region
- Centralized inbox with conversation threading and follow-up automation
- Response milestones: Idle, Negotiation, Won, Lost
- Personalized pitch templates with context fields
- Engagement metrics: open rates, reply rates
- Shared blacklist across campaigns
- Campaign tracker grouped by client, brand, campaign

## Included modules
- HARO Automation: matches brand expertise with press queries, sends verified responses, tracks publication outcomes
- QUEST PR Finder: identifies publishers and domains referenced by LLMs (ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity) for LLM-aware targeting

## Scope
- Link building outreach
- Digital PR and earned media
- LLM-driven discovery targeting
