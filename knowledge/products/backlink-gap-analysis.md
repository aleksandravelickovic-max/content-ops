## Definition
Backlink Gap Analysis (also called Link Gap Analysis Tool) is an AI competitive link intelligence system that identifies domains linking to competitors but not to the target site. Compares up to 6 domains.

## How it works
Compares backlink profiles, referring domains, and spam scores. Ranks link opportunities by domain authority (DA), trust signals, and link type. AI prioritization suggests link targets most likely to drive ranking impact, relevance alignment, and E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness) growth.

## Core capabilities
- Multi-domain backlink comparison (up to 6 domains)
- Referring domain identification across competitors, excluding the primary site
- Sortable results with columns for DA, spam score, and top linking pages per competitor
- AI-prioritized target list for outreach
- Direct export for campaign use
