## Definition
Domain Power is a proprietary 100-point logarithmic authority metric developed in Search Atlas. Measures true organic visibility and competitive strength by integrating organic traffic, keyword performance, and ranking distribution across the top 100 SERPs.

## How it works
Aggregates live keyword data, backlink trust, and topical relevance into a single correlated score. Continuously recalculated through Site Explorer infrastructure, synchronized with real-time SERP monitoring and LLM Visibility pipelines.

## Key differentiators
- Cannot be manipulated through volume-based link schemes because it measures organic presence and topical coverage
- Replaces link-based estimates with real visibility signals
- Aligns more closely with Google ranking behavior than DA or DR

## Use cases
- Prioritize backlink opportunities
- Evaluate acquisition targets
- Benchmark authority growth
