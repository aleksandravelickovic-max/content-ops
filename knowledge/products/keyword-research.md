## Definition
Keyword Research in Search Atlas is an AI-augmented SEO and PPC keyword intelligence system that analyzes keyword performance, intent, and competition.

## How it works
Displays real-time metrics for each keyword: search volume (SV), keyword difficulty (KD), cost per click (CPC), and topical search volume (TSV). Connects to SERP Overview for analysis of top-ranking pages, domain diversity, backlink profiles, and organic traffic values.

## Core capabilities
- Access to over 5.2 billion keywords
- Exact-match terms (precise query strings, verbatim searches)
- Long-tail variations (extended phrases, question-based queries, conversational searches)
- Question-based queries (who, what, where, when, why, how)
- Historical search volume visualization
- Mobile vs. desktop traffic breakdown
- Global keyword distribution across countries
- SERP Overview: top-ranking pages, domain diversity, backlink profiles, traffic values

## Integrations
- Content Genius (Content Assistant) for keyword-to-content workflows
- Direct send of selected keywords to content generation or optimization modules
