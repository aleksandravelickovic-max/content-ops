## Definition
Bulk URL Analyzer (Free DA Checker) is a Search Atlas feature that measures Domain Authority and Page Authority across multiple domains in one operation.

## How it works
Runs stable batch requests through direct API access. Delivers results across thousands of domains without crashes or incomplete data.

## Measurements
- Domain Authority (DA): overall link equity
- Page Authority (PA): URL-level strength

## Use cases
- Evaluate link-building prospects at scale
- Assess acquisition targets
- Select partners
- Compare large prospect lists (hundreds of URLs)
