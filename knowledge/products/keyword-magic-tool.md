## Definition
Keyword Magic Tool is an AI keyword expansion engine in Search Atlas that accelerates keyword discovery through semantic analysis.

## How it works
Analyzes any seed keyword and generates semantically related terms: long-tail variations, alternative phrasings, and topic-adjacent ideas. Each suggestion carries search volume, cost-per-click, PPC difficulty, and intent classification (informational, navigational, transactional, commercial).

## Core capabilities
- Seed-to-cluster semantic expansion
- Instant filtering by region, device, or intent
- Automatic grouping of semantically related keywords into clusters aligned with topic trees
- One-click send of clusters to content strategies or articles

## Integrations
- Topical Map Generator
- Content Genius
