## Definition
Site Explorer is an enterprise-level SEO intelligence platform inside Search Atlas that performs domain-level, subdomain-level, and URL-level analysis across organic, paid, and backlink datasets.

## How it works
Uses proprietary data pipelines, real-time SERP synchronization, and the Domain Power metric (100-point logarithmic authority benchmark) to quantify website performance. Organizes metrics around four Holistic SEO pillars: Experience, Authority, Content, Technical.

## Core capabilities
- Organic keywords, paid keywords, traffic metrics, SERP feature share
- Backlink quality, referring domains, keyword intent segmentation
- Organic and paid traffic breakdowns by country
- Historical keyword position trends
- Branded vs. non-branded traffic share
- Domain metrics: Trust Flow, Citation Flow, Spam Score, URL Rating

## Integrated modules
- Keyword Gap Analysis
- Backlink Gap Analysis
- Topical Dominance
- LLM Visibility
