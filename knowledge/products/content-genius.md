## Definition
Content Genius is an AI-based content editor that engineers SEO copy through semantic, structural, and intent modeling. Applies NLP, entity recognition, and contextual clustering.

## How it works
Integrates real-time SERP extraction, entity-weighted keyword research, and passage-level optimization. Each output includes keyword placement control, semantic vector scoring, and paragraph flow validation.

## Operational modes
- **Write Yourself**: manual drafting with optimization overlays
- **Write With AI**: collaborative content generation guided by AI scoring
- **Write With AI in Bulk**: automated production for scaled publishing

## Core capabilities
- AI Folders for brand rules, tone presets, and language consistency
- Live Content Score panels (keyword density, heading coverage, contextual completeness)
- Entity frequency alignment, lexical diversity, sentence structure scoring
- Passage indexing and Helpful Content alignment

## Integrations
- Keyword Research
- SCHOLAR
- Topical Map Generator
- Meta Generator
