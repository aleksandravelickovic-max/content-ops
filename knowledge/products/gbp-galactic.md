## Definition
GBP Galactic (Google Business Profile Galactic) is an AI local SEO automation system inside Search Atlas that manages, optimizes, and scales Google Business Profiles across multiple locations.

## How it works
Synchronizes business data, updates listings, responds to reviews, and automates Q&A and schema markup updates in real time. Connects directly to Google Maps, data aggregator networks, and the Search Atlas Local SEO Heatmap for continuous performance monitoring.

## Core capabilities
- Review management and response automation with AI-written, editable replies
- Q&A generation for anticipating user questions
- Profile completion tracking via the "My Tasks" module (attributes, images, service updates)
- Real-time listing synchronization across all business locations
- Service area definition and update automation for non-storefront businesses
- Social media integration: auto-sharing GBP posts on Facebook and Instagram

## Goals
- Improve NAP consistency (Name, Address, Phone)
- Strengthen reputation signals
- Maintain visibility in competitive local markets

## Integrations
- Google Maps
- Data aggregator networks: Data Axle, Neustar, Foursquare, YP Network, GPS
- Search Atlas citation management
- Search Atlas Local SEO Heatmap
- Search Atlas ranking tools
