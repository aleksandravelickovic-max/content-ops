## Definition
Local SEO Heatmaps visualize the organic visibility of a business across geographic areas by tracking Google Map Pack rankings for specific keywords and service regions.

## How it works
Pulls live data from local results. Each pin represents a physical search location, with position values showing whether the business ranks in the Map Pack or lower SERP tiers.

## Access path
GBP Galactic → Local SEO Tools → Heatmaps

## Configuration
- Region, radius, and shape (circle or rectangle)
- Up to 7 layers or 175 pins
- Refresh cadence: daily, weekly, or monthly
- Device segmentation: desktop or mobile

## Core capabilities
- Smart Suggest: AI-recommended local keywords from business category and GBP data
- Click-pin visibility inspection
- Competitor comparison view
- Ranking change tracking over time
- Standardized radius, pin spacing, refresh timing across reports
- Public URL sharing, dashboard embedding, PDF export

## Integrations
- GBP Galactic
- GSC Performance
- Site Explorer
- White-Label SEO
