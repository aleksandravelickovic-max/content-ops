## Definition
Content Rewriting (Content Rewriter Quick Tool) is an AI-based linguistic transformation feature in Search Atlas that reconstructs existing text through controlled tone, syntactic variation, and semantic accuracy.

## How it works
Applies multi-layered NLP modeling: lexical substitution, sentence reformation, contextual coherence. Preserves factual density, topical relevance, and search intent.

## Core capabilities
- Tone of Voice Engine with predefined profiles (professional, casual, persuasive)
- Creative presets (Yoda, Tony Stark, Shakespeare)
- Configurable language and point-of-view (first, second, third person)
- Algorithmic scoring for semantic alignment, readability, contextual fluency before export

## Integrations
- SCHOLAR grading
- Content Genius
