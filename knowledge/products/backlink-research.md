## Definition
Backlink Research is a link intelligence dashboard that evaluates the authority, quality, and trajectory of a domain's backlink profile.

## How it works
Combines Domain Authority (DA), Page Authority (PA), spam score, and linking velocity into a unified report powered by live web crawls and AI signal mapping.

## Interface sections
- Linking Domains
- Linking Pages
- New and Lost Domains
- Top Pages
- Anchor Texts
- Spam Links

## Core capabilities
- Monitor new vs. lost links, dofollow ratios, anchor text trends, domain-level trust distribution
- Growth pattern and link health graphs across time
- Filters for specific sources, link types, geographic clusters

## Use cases
- Monitor backlink health (profile quality, link sustainability, authority stability)
- Detect spammy or toxic signals (harmful links, negative SEO, risky associations)
- Locate new link acquisition opportunities
- Competitive benchmarking: analyze where other sites earn links
