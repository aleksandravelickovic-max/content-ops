## Definition
Smart Ads is an AI PPC automation system that builds, optimizes, and manages Google Ads campaigns inside the Search Atlas dashboard. Powered by Atlas Brain, Smart Ads translates goals into campaign actions, reallocates budget to what converts, and deploys optimizations directly inside live campaigns. Previously named OTTO Google Ads, and before that OTTO PPC.

## How it works
Atlas Brain identifies products, services, and search intent by analyzing a connected website and Google Ads account. It creates structured campaigns aligned with user goals and ROAS (return on ad spend) targets, executes audits and optimizations across campaigns, and keeps humans in control through approval checkpoints.

## Core capabilities
- Campaign structure and keyword clustering using Single-Themed Ad Groups (STAG)
- Ad copy generation based on audience intent, CPC, and conversion data
- Budget recommendations and reallocation tuned to performance metrics and ROI goals
- Automatic negative keyword filtering to remove low-quality traffic
- AI retargeting campaigns for re-engaging non-converting visitors
- Campaign audits with a health score across keywords, creatives, and spend patterns
- Direct execution of optimizations through Atlas Brain
- Conversion value control that syncs back into Google Ads for value-based bidding

## Operating modes (through Atlas Brain)
- **Fast mode**: minimal setup, speed-first
- **Advanced mode**: step-by-step approvals, tighter governance

## Workflow coverage
- Paid search
- Retargeting
- Display
- Remarketing
- Imported campaigns

## Control and visibility
- Campaign Hub: centralizes campaigns across businesses and campaign types
- Campaign Performance: granular marketer view with direct edits
- Report Section: executive-grade reporting with customizable metrics and date ranges
- Users review, adjust, and relaunch campaigns directly in Search Atlas

## Integrations
- Atlas Brain (agentic execution layer)
- Google Tag Manager (GTM)
- Google Ads API
- Report Builder
