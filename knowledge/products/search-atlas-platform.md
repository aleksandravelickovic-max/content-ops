## Definition
Search Atlas is an AI-powered digital marketing platform that centralizes SEO, GEO (Generative Engine Optimization), AEO (Answer Engine Optimization), and LLM Visibility functions in one system. Powered by OTTO, its proprietary execution engine.

## How it works
Processes live signals from search engines (Google, Bing, Yahoo), map networks (Google Maps, Apple Maps, Bing Places), and large language models (ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity) to execute technical remediation, content production, and authority validation inside one AI environment.

## Core capabilities
- Keyword research (discovery, volume analysis, competition assessment)
- Technical audits (site health monitoring, crawlability testing, indexation validation)
- Backlink strategy (link quality analysis, prospect identification, outreach automation)
- Local SEO (GBP management, citation building, geographic ranking tracking)
- AI-assisted content creation (semantic optimization, SERP alignment, automated publishing)
- AI-guided automation that suggests actions, implements changes, and monitors results

## Key differentiators
- Executes optimization tasks directly through OTTO, rather than only measuring them
- Unified interface replaces fragmented tool stacks (Semrush, Ahrefs, Surfer SEO, and similar)
- Proprietary metrics: Domain Power, Topical Dominance

## Target audience
- Agencies (multi-client management, bulk operations, white-label reporting)
- In-house marketers (website optimization, content strategy, performance tracking)
- Brands (organic traffic growth, search visibility improvement, competitive analysis)
