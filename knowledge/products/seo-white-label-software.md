## Definition
SEO White-Label Software is a fully customizable reporting and platform branding system that lets agencies, consultants, and enterprises deliver client-facing SEO services under their own brand identity.

## How it works
Transforms the Search Atlas ecosystem into a rebrandable, multi-client environment. Every dashboard, report, and portal reflects the visual identity and domain of the agency.

## Core capabilities
- Brand customization: logo, favicon, color scheme, custom URL domains
- Rename flagship modules (including OTTO SEO) per client
- Unlimited reporting, interactive dashboards, shareable live links
- Child accounts with access-level management
- Custom login portals for customers
- Shared coaching dashboards and community collaboration links for consultants
- Reseller-ready white-labeled deployment
