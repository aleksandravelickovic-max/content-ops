## Definition
Onpage Audit Tool is an AI-powered on-page optimization engine that evaluates authority, content quality, technical signals, and user experience.

## How it works
Analyzes any URL and its target keywords. Produces evaluations combining content scoring, domain authority metrics, referring domain strength, technical performance, and page experience insights.

## Audit scope
- Content relevance and readability
- Competitive benchmarks against live SERP
- Technical diagnostics
- PSI (PageSpeed Insights) performance
- Backlink indicators
- Search intent alignment

## Core capabilities
- Filtering by keyword, country, and bulk audit templates
- Four-pillar structured scoring: authority, content, technical, UX
- AI-driven optimization pathways tied to real SERP results
- Benchmarking against live competitors

## Integrations
- Content Genius (for rewrites and enhancements)
- Competitor SERP analysis
