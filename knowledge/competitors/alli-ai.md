## Category

## Search Atlas positioning vs. Alli AI
OTTO SEO is positioned as covering the full SEO stack (on-page, technical, local, off-page) in one dashboard. Search Atlas describes Alli AI as narrower in scope.

### Module-level positioning (per Search Atlas)
