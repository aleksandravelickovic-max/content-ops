## Category
SEO suite with Keyword Explorer and Link Explorer as flagship modules.

## Search Atlas positioning vs. Moz
### Module-level positioning (per Search Atlas)
- Moz Keyword Explorer vs. Search Atlas Keyword Research: no global regional filters, traffic potential modeling, or LLM-powered keyword clustering.
- Moz Link Explorer vs. Search Atlas Backlink Research: no integrated domain authority scoring with spam detection and live link velocity analytics across multiple timeframes.
- Moz Link Explorer vs. Search Atlas Backlink Profile Comparison: no real-time authority segmentation, link diversity visualization, or one-click Report Builder export.
- Moz Domain Authority vs. Domain Power: estimated link quality tiers rather than SERP-verified keyword reach and ranking correlation.
- Moz On-Page Grader vs. On-page Audit: no live competitor benchmarking, keyword-specific content depth, or AI-driven optimization paths tied to real SERP results.
- Moz Bulk DA Checker vs. Bulk URL Analyzer: no live backlink freshness scoring or verified link trust weighting at scale.
- Moz Link Explorer vs. Site Explorer: static link indices and limited keyword segmentation; no high-frequency recrawling or full SERP feature tracking.
