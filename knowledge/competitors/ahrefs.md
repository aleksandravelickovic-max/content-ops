## Category
All-in-one SEO platform with Keywords Explorer, Content Gap, Link Intersect, Domain Comparison, and Site Audit.

## Search Atlas positioning vs. Ahrefs
Positioned as report-first: Ahrefs surfaces data; Search Atlas combines data with AI-driven execution and publisher outreach automation.

### Module-level positioning (per Search Atlas)
- Ahrefs vs. OTTO SEO: static crawl reports; no continuous technical optimization or live schema injection.
- Ahrefs Keywords Explorer vs. Search Atlas Keyword Research: static reports; no live traffic modeling or historical SERP context combining organic and paid.
- Ahrefs Content Gap vs. Search Atlas Keyword Gap Tool: no live ranking data, CTR modeling, or intent-weighted opportunity scoring.
- Ahrefs Link Intersect vs. Search Atlas Backlink Gap: no integrated spam scoring, DA filtering, or automated outreach.
- Ahrefs Domain Comparison vs. Search Atlas Backlink Profile Comparison: no continuous spam scoring, anchor categorization, or link velocity analytics over time.

### Additional modules (from features 14-39 coverage)
- Ahrefs Site Explorer vs. Search Atlas Site Explorer: no Domain Power traffic-weighted authority; Ahrefs link graph size is susceptible to inflation.
- Ahrefs Domain Rating vs. Domain Power: no real-traffic and keyword factoring; static link graph size only.
- Ahrefs Rank Tracker vs. Keyword Rank Tracker: no GA4 traffic correlation tying ranking data to actual clicks and impressions.
- Ahrefs Alerts vs. Universal Email Alerts: backlinks and rankings only; no authority, indexing, or topical coverage signals in a unified feed.
- Ahrefs Dashboards vs. Report Builder: single-source (Ahrefs data only) rather than multi-source with daily refreshes.
- Ahrefs Content Explorer vs. Content Velocity: indexed pages only; no sitemap publishing velocity.
- Ahrefs Site Audit vs. Crawl Monitoring: simulated crawlers only; no real search engine bot activity tracking.
- Ahrefs Site Audit Content Analysis vs. On-page Audit: crawl-health focus; no unified content + authority + UX + technical scoring.
- Ahrefs Content Reports vs. Content Pruning: simple "low traffic" flags; no Content Genius-linked pruning workflows.
- Ahrefs Batch Analysis vs. Bulk URL Analyzer: static link graph calculations without real keyword visibility and traffic-based authority modeling.
- Ahrefs vs. Portfolio Summary: no GA4, GSC, GBP, and ranking integration; depends on Ahrefs crawls alone.
