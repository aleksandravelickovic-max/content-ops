## Category
Local SEO reporting platform with citation management and review monitoring.

## Search Atlas positioning vs. BrightLocal
GBP Galactic integrates real-time listing updates, AI-generated content, and service area automation inside one dashboard. Search Atlas describes BrightLocal as dependent on batch reporting cycles.

### Additional modules
- BrightLocal Heatmaps vs. Local SEO Heatmaps: no dynamic heatmaps linked to live GBP data, automated refresh schedules, or Smart Suggest keyword generation.
- BrightLocal Citations vs. Local Citation Builder: requires manual entry; no simultaneous submission across all five major data aggregators.
