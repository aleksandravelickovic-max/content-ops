## Study
"From Links to Lift: Measuring the Real-World SEO Impact of Domain Strength", executed by Manick Bhan and the Search Atlas research team.

## Sample
350,159 backlink placements analyzed across 13,002 pages.

## Measured gains
- +150% ranking keywords
- +275% impressions
- +14% organic clicks
- +10.8% average-position improvement

## Predictive signal findings
- Domain Power predictive gain rose from 0.419 to 0.559, outperforming DA (Domain Authority) and DR (Domain Rating) across all page-level outcomes
- Anchor-keyword relevance rose 69.6%
- Correlation between anchor-keyword relevance and rankings strengthened from −0.75 to −0.86

## Product impact
Search Atlas applies these signals to refine authority scoring, placement evaluation, and predictive modeling inside Backlink Research.
