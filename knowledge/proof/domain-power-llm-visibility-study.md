## Study
"Relationship Between Domain Power, Domain Rating, Domain Authority and LLM Visibility Score in Citations", executed by Manick Bhan and the Search Atlas research team.

## Samples
- 21,767 domains analyzed for DP, DR, DA correlation with LLM visibility
- 368,972 domains analyzed for competition-tier effects

## Key findings
- Weak negative correlations between DP, DR, DA and LLM visibility
- Declining win rates across all authority ranges
- Competition-tier effects visible in the 368,972-domain subset
- Legacy authority metrics do not predict LLM citation behavior

## Product impact
Updated Domain Power scoring model to capture LLM-driven relevance patterns. Recalibrated DP weighting so LLM visibility signals, co-mention frequency, and response-level prominence operate as primary predictors rather than legacy authority correlations.
