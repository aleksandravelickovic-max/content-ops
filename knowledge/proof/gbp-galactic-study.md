## Study
"Sector-Specific Ranking Dynamics in Local SEO: A Machine Learning Perspective", executed by Manick Bhan and the Search Atlas research team.

## Sample and method
- 7,718 businesses modeled across 676 sectors
- XGBoost models reaching R² = 0.80 to 0.98

## Key findings
- `distance_kmeters` dominated ranking influence, contributing up to 70% of variance
- Review volume, keyword relevance, and GBP profile relevance collectively explained most remaining variance across industries

## Product impact
GBP Galactic now applies these high-impact signals to recalibrated scoring, prioritization, and automation rules. Optimization pathways align with the strongest universally observed local ranking factors.
