## Study
"How GPT Results Differ from Search Engine Results", produced by Manick Bhan and the Search Atlas research team.

## Sample
18,377 semantically matched LLM–SERP query pairs. Alignment gaps quantified across domains and URLs.

## Key findings
- Perplexity: 25 to 30% median domain overlap with SERPs; 20% median URL overlap
- GPT and Gemini: below 15% domain overlap; below 10% URL overlap
- Distinct retrieval and reasoning behaviors between platforms require dedicated visibility tracking

## Product impact
The LLM Visibility system classifies overlap profiles at the pair, platform, and intent levels. It processes normalized URL and domain signatures and computes distribution-grade overlap vectors that reflect true retrieval fidelity across search engines and generative engines.
