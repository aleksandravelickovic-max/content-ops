## Study
"Quantifying the Value of OTTO Automation in Google Ads Performance", conducted by Manick Bhan and the Search Atlas research team. The study measures the OTTO automation layer that now powers Smart Ads.

## Sample
Tens of thousands of campaigns analyzed across CTR and CPC distributions before and after the April 22, 2025 update.

## Key measured results
- Smart Ads average CTR: 6.8% vs. Google 5.9%
- Smart Ads median CTR: 5.7% vs. Google 4.8%
- ΔCTR pre-update: +17.86%
- ΔCTR post-update: +10.46%
- CPC reduction: from $5.87 to $5.34 (−9.04%) after the strategy shift
- Non-parametric significance (Wilcoxon): p < 2.2e-16

## Product impact
Engineering recalibrated keyword-generation pipelines, bidding heuristics, and ad-variant selection logic based on these results. Reduced CPC volatility and delivered a more predictable Atlas Brain automation layer for Smart Ads.
