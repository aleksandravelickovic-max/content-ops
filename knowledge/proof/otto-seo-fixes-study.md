## Study
"Automated Technical SEO Fixes: What's the SEO Impact", led by Manick Bhan and the Search Atlas research team.

## Sample
39,876 websites analyzed across impressions, traffic, keywords, CTR, and average position.

## Key measured lifts
- +150.5% schema-related impression gain
- +114.3% gain from missing-heading fixes
- Median +146 long-term impressions per site
- Median +67 additional keywords per site
- Median CTR increase of 0.000294 (approximately +0.03 percentage points)
- Median ranking improvement of 2 positions

## Big cluster subset
Big clusters gained:
- +2,370 median impressions
- Substantial CTR and position improvements at scale

## Product impact
Search Atlas updated OTTO SEO to prioritize the structural remedies that produced the highest effect sizes: schema markup, heading hierarchy, canonical consolidation, meta keywords, and H1 optimization.
