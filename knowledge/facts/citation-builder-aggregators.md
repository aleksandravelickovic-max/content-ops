Local Citation Builder distributes business data to 5 major data aggregator networks:

1. Data Axle
2. Foursquare
3. Neustar Localeze
4. Yellow Pages Network
5. GPS Network

## Cost
- 30 Hyperdrive credits per aggregator
- 120 Hyperdrive credits for all five aggregators
- Includes 12 months of free updates without additional credit deductions
