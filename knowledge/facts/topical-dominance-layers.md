Topical Dominance evaluates entity coverage, content depth, and ranking breadth across 3 layers of increasing topical scope:

1. **Specific Topic**: individual keyword groups
2. **Broad Topic**: adjacent semantic clusters
3. **Knowledge Domain**: entire semantic networks

Scored from 0 to 100, derived from content volume, topical relevance, and publishing consistency.
