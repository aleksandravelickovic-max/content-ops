SCHOLAR evaluates content across 12 dimensions:

1. Overall Score
2. Content Clarity
3. Factuality
4. Human Effort
5. Information Gain
6. Content Freshness
7. User Intent
8. Entity Score
9. Contextual Flow
10. Numerical Content
11. Query Relevance
12. Readability
